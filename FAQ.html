Please refer to our frequently asked questions below to find answers to common questions regarding our services.

How do I place an order with Apex?

To place an order, simply navigate over to the Start a Project and fill in the required information concerning your particular project.

I have extra files I would like to submit for the writer, where can I do that?

If you have extra files that would help the writer complete your project, you will have the option to upload the files at the end of the Start a Project page

My order requires me to use a specific source that I do not have, can you guys find it?

We have a wide variety of online resources and access to multiple databases to find the right source for you. While we can't find absolutely everything, we can typically dig it out. If a situation arises where we can't find a certain book or article, we will notify you ASAP and figure out the best solution.

I am curious about the status of my project, how do I find out?

If you would like an update concerning the status of your project, please send us an email from the Contact page.

What if I am not satisfied with my project?

If for some reason you are not completely satisfied with the quality of the delivered project, you may ask for a free revision according to our Revision Policy within fourteen (14) days after the actual deadline (the moment that we have sent you your completed paper) of your order. The writer will adjust everything according to your comments. However, revision instructions should not violate the original ones.

What happens if I need last minute revisions made to my project?

While we make our best effort to be responsive and helpful 24/7, all changes must be made in accordance with our Revision Policy. If you would like this policy explained to you in greater detail, please contact us at info@apex-resourcing.com

I need my paper really soon! When will I get it?

You will receive your paper by the time and date specified.

How will I get my project?

All orders are emailed to you the moment they are complete.

What are the proofreading and editing services?

Proofreading and editing services are necessary when you already have a draft or the whole project completed and would like our writers to improve its content. Both these services will require you to email the initial text since you are not paying for writing from scratch. Editing and proofreading are defined as changing the content of your draft, formatting the project according to a particular formatting style, and proofreading the content. Editing and proofreading may cover a possible change of content of up to 25-30%.

All of our writers are seasoned veterans of the trade. They all went to prestigious universities and graduated with high GPA's. They know the ins and outs of how to get A's.

What is your revision policy?

We will be happy to revise your order for free as long as the revision you request fits the scope of your original order. Please note that you have fourteen (14) days after the actual deadline (the moment that we have sent you your completed paper) of your order to ask for a free revision according to our Revision Policy.

Is your service confidential?

Yes, we are a private company and uphold a strong sense of decorum in dealing with confidentiality. We will not, under any circumstance (unless required to do so by federal law), give out any of your personal information to anyone, ever.
